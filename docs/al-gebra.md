# Al-gebra

*27. kesäkuuta 2026*

## I

$$x^3 - 4x^2 - 9x + 36 = 0$$

$$x^2(x-4) - 9(x-4) = 0$$

$$(x^2-9)(x-4) = 0$$

$$(x+3)(x-3)(x-4) = 0$$

$$x+3=0 \qquad x-3=0 \qquad x-4=0$$

$$x=-3 \qquad\quad x=3 \qquad\quad x=4$$

---

## II

$$x^4 - 13x^2 + 36 = 0$$

$$o = x^2$$

$$o^2 - 13o + 36 = 0$$

$$o = \frac{-(-13) \pm \sqrt{(-13)^2 - 4 \cdot 1 \cdot 36}}{2 \cdot 1}$$

$$o = \frac{13 \pm 5}{2}$$

$$o = 9 \qquad\qquad o = 4$$

$$x^2 = 9 \qquad\quad x^2 = 4$$

$$x = \pm 3 \qquad\quad x = \pm 2$$

---

## III

$$2x^3 - 3x^2 + 8x - 12 = 0$$

$$x^2(2x-3) + 4(2x-3) = 0$$

$$(x^2+4)(2x-3) = 0$$

$$x^2 + 4 = 0$$

$$x^2 = -4$$

$$x = \pm\sqrt{-4} = \pm\sqrt{4}\cdot\sqrt{-1} = \pm 2i$$

$$2x - 3 = 0 \implies x = \frac{3}{2}$$

---

## IV

$$x^3 - 2x^2 - 5x + 6 = 0$$

Mahdolliset juuret: \(\pm 1,\ \pm 2,\ \pm 3,\ \pm 6\)

$$1^3 - 2 \cdot 1^2 - 5 \cdot 1 + 6 = 0$$

\((x-1)\) on juuri. Jaetaan synteettisesti:

$$
\begin{array}{r|rrrr}
1 & 1 & -2 & -5 & 6 \\
  &   &  1 & -1 & -6 \\
\hline
  & 1 & -1 & -6 & 0
\end{array}
$$

$$x^2 - x - 6 = (x-3)(x+2)$$

$$(x-1)(x-3)(x+2) = 0$$

$$x-1=0 \qquad x-3=0 \qquad x+2=0$$

$$x=1 \qquad\quad x=3 \qquad\quad x=-2$$
